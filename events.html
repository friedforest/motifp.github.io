We all share a common home - Earth, an extremely diverse one.

Thousands or even millions of different cultures exist in the world, though we might not realize it. There are 3,000 ethnicities in Africa, whose people speak over 2,000 languages. China has 56 ethnic groups, with Han being the largest ethnicity in the world, whose population makes up 92% of the total population of the world.

Each ethnic group has their own unique culture, this forms our diverse society. However, the diversity is a double-edged sword. Conflicts often occur when one’s value clashes with other people from a different culture. Globally, the conflicts arose from the cultural difference are getting more and more serious, and they even impact the relationship between countries.

As a citizen, we are unlikely to influence the political decision of our government, but what we can do is to appreciate different cultures, and music aids this process. The music we like or create are often influenced by our culture, and the exchange of musical ideas between musicians from different cultures is a way to share different cultures, and learning to understanding them.
